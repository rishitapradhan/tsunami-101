Emergency Kit Checklist

  • Map of area showing an agreed meeting spot and nearest safe shelter

    Family and emergency contact information

    Basic first aid kit

    Water and non perishable food (three to seven day supply)

    Flashlight

    Battery powered radio

    Extra batteries

    Medical supplies and medications

    Extra cash

    Blankets/bedding

    Personal hygiene items (soap, toothpaste, toothbrush, hand wipes, sanitizer)

    Copies of important personal documents in waterproof container (proof of address, insurance policy in, birth certificate, passports, proof of address)

    Cell phones with chargers
